## What Helps Me

- Clear structure and templates
- Evidence-based recommendations
- Explicit tradeoffs stated
- Next actions always clear
- Links to sources

## What Frustrates Me

- Vague recommendations without evidence
- Long-winded explanations (get to the point)
- Missing context (always tell me why)
- Overly cautious hedging (give me your actual opinion)
- Surprises in meetings (prep me in advance)

## Response Preferences by Situation

### When I ask "What's next?"
Give me: Top 3 prioritized actions aligned to COMPASS goals, with brief rationale

### When I ask for analysis
Give me: Decision-first summary, then supporting evidence in tables

### When I ask for help with writing
Give me: Draft in my communication style, ready to send with minor edits

### When I hit a blocker
Give me: Options to resolve, tradeoffs of each, recommended path

### When I need to present to leadership
Give me: Executive summary format, metrics-focused, risks visible
