## Challenge Patterns

### Recurring Themes
1. **Information gaps** - Need better access to customer data, competitive intel
2. **Capacity constraints** - Team stretched between KTLO and growth
3. **Legacy burden** - Technical debt slows everything

### What Works
- Building relationships with Sales/CS for customer access
- Using PM OS to systematize work
- Evidence-based arguments with leadership

### What Doesn't Work
- Assuming data exists (it usually doesn't)
- Big bang transformation plans (too much resistance)
- Working in isolation (need cross-functional buy-in)
