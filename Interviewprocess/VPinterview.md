### Active Catalog Understanding & AI Vision

- Current challenges identified:
  - 3x more SKUs since 2020
  - 5-7 new retail channels opened
  - Manual supplier onboarding with unstructured data (CSV, PDF, images)
  - Launch cycles reduced from months to weeks
- AI use cases proposed:
  1. Multimodal data extraction to accelerate supplier onboarding
  2. Semantic search (e.g., “black” matching “blk” in supplier catalogs)
- Phased implementation approach:
  - Start with non-AI tools (OCR) to validate value
  - Decision checkpoint before AI investment
  - Focus on measurable outcomes (6% onboarding time reduction target)

### Product Strategy Presentation

- Vision: Accelerate onboarding through AI + enable real-time transaction validation
- Two-phase roadmap:
  - Phase 1: Discovery, customer interviews, prototype validation
  - Phase 2: Proprietary AI models and real-time validation
- First 90 days:
  - Customer interviews and usage data audit
  - Vision document and product charter alignment
  - Hypothesis validation and technology selection
- Success metrics:
  - Reduced chargebacks for retailers
  - Faster time-to-publish for suppliers
  - Network adoption enabling land-and-expand
